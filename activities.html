School Co-Curriculum Activities

    Games and School Functions :

  • Besides the school curriculum, great importance is also attached to the co-curricular activities such as classwise, housewise,inter-school wise competitions in games, dramatics, debates, elocution, painting, sports and creative S.U.P.W projects. Within the school the competitive items lead to the Annual Sports Day, and the cultural items to the Annual Concert Day. Guided study tours are organized under supervision of the school staff according to the means and the convenience of the school.
  • Children selected for Dramatics, Elocution, Debates-Arts & Craft, Essay, Story Writing Competitions and S.U.P.W. projects must willingly make time for such school activities.
  • Participation in Games and Physical Training is compulsory. When matches are arranged, either on interclass basis, interhouse basis or interschool basis, students should wear term colours and represent their class, house or school at such meets. Students should make time to attend the practice sessions.
  • Students will be allotted house to have intramural Housewise league competitions. Each competitive meet will carry points for the House and the house amassing the highest number of points will win the honours and the Cock House shield.
  • Parents are requested to encourage their children to participate in these activities by being present at the activities of their children when invited. When students are selected to represent their team, but do not do so, then they will be penalized and debarred from further participation in school activities.
