About Us

Rajpoot global School, Hardoi is an English Medium School. It is a Christan Minority Institution, established and administered by the Catholic Diocese Of Lucknow, which has its headquarters in Lucknow. It is owned by the Catholic Diocese of Lucknow, a registered Religious and Charitable Society, registered in Lucknow.

It is owned by the Catholic Diocese of Lucknow, a registered Religious and Charitable Society, registered under the Societi -es Act XXI of 1860, registered in Lucknow on the 24th November, 1949.
